AntiSLAPP: Legal Defender

Pro bono legal aid

AntiSLAPP Legal Defender is a proof-of-concept for an AI-enhanced robot lawyer. It is designed to help you put together a statement of defence when you’ve been sued for defamation.

To get started, type “hello” in the conversation box on the right. The AI will lead you through a series of questions to put together a defence. When it’s finished putting together a statement for you, you’ll receive a link to the statement to download.

Don’t worry about fixing any typos or minor mistakes. You can either ask the AI the restart the process (from the beginning) or just edit the statement when it’s done.

One last point: this project is a work in progress and cannot yet replace professional legal advice. It has been designed from the perspective of Canadian common law. Consider its conclusions to be advice, and the statement it draws up a starting point.
